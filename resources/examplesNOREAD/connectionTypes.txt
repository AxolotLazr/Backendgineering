Conection types


1. belt
- for solid items
- connects to belts
2. wire
- for electricity
3. vent
- only output
- for gasses
- default: open; can be manually closed
4. chute
- for solids or gasses
- can dump out on belts or be dumped into from belts
5. valve
- for liquids or gasses
- connects to pipes
- automatic, manual
